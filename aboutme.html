Introduction.

My name is Brian Meder. I am a computer science major at the University of Scranton. My interests in computing are web development, cybersecurity, and game design. I started coding 3 years ago and have since built skills in Java, C, Assembly, HTML, CSS, Javascript, the Bootstrap framework, and APIs. I am soon to be inducted into the Pennsylvania Gamma Chapter of Upsilon Pi Epsilon, The International Honor Society for the Computing and Information Disciplines, an honor in which I was nominated by the computing sciences faculty at my university. Outside of computing, I enjoy music and play several instruments.

About this webpage

I have coded this entire portfolio website myself, with the help of Youtube and the Global Tech Experience summer web development course.
